About

This album explores the intricate art and craftsmanship behind luxury watches, drawing inspiration from the precision, elegance, and timeless appeal of horology. Each track mirrors the ticking of a finely-tuned timepiece, weaving together rhythms that evoke the meticulous engineering and heritage of iconic watch brands. From classic designs to modern innovations, the music captures the essence of luxury, blending time and sound in perfect harmony.
